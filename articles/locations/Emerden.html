Emerden

A small, quiet, and mostly unknown, seaside hamlet in the region of Imas'bhenadaar, in the N'shanal'afaar peninsula, built around a huge tree. The races live together mostly in harmony.

Demographics

The hamlet is populated by mostly humans, with them making up two thirds of the population, with the rest being a mix of 4 Half-Elves, 2 Elves and a Dwarf. A third of the people are female, with the settlement consisting of 14 males. About half of the people don't have any occupation's as they are either elderly or children, though that doesn't stop them from helping the rest of their neighbours when they can. The majority of the people are fishermen, since the climate isn't proper for agriculture to be a sustainable food source.
